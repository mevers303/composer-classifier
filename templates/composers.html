Composers we recognize

Welcome to the web interface for Who wrote that song?. Who wrote that song? is a Recurrent Neural Network that has been trained to recognize the varying musial styles of various classical composers by analyzing their sheet music. It was written in Python 3.6 using TensorFlow with Keras a wrapper.
