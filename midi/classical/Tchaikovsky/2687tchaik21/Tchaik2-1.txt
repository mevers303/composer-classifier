   This sequence is of the first movement of Tchaikovsky's 2nd
Symphony, based on Ukrainian themes. It was sequenced for the
Proteus 2 sound module.

   All tracks have been transposed to concert pitch.

   Note that there are two program changes in the French
Horn track, in bars 2 and 70. Program 36 is a p French
Horn, 37 is ff, and 38 is a French Horn section.

   String sections share their corresponding MIDI channel
with program changes between bowed and pizzicato strings.
